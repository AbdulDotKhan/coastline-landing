What is Coastline?

Coastline is an integrated e-commerce and inventory management platform for the fishing industry. With the rise of retained-ownership fisheries and due the legal requirements of the chain of command system, fisheries have an increasing need to track and ensure the traceability of their seafood inventory.

This is where Coastline comes in. Coastline's automated workflow eliminates the need for manual data re-entry, with set conversion algorithms tracking the weight, catch date, location, and product-state changes for the fishery. Further, the platform provides fisheries a online market to publish their completed seafood inventory, facilitating direct sales partnerships with local restaurants, grocery chains, and end consumers.

Key Data & Definitions

Industry Size: $11.6 billion CDN (Canada & US)

Target Market Size: $500 Million (Canada & US)

Market Trends: CSFs and Retained Ownership Fisheries have grown 100% per year over the past 5 years

Community Supported Fishery: A community-supported fishery (CSF) is an alternative business model for selling fresh, locally sourced seafood. CSF programs, modeled after increasingly popular community-supported agriculture programs, offer members weekly shares of fresh seafood for a pre-paid membership fee. (Wikipedia)

Retained Ownership Model: A rising trend in which fisheries contract out services (processing, storing, packaging) to intermediaries, retain ownership through the value chain, and sell the finished good at a higher margin.
