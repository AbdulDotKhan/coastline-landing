Coastline's Vision

Coastline is a software platform built for the fishing industry. Ultimately, Coastline will connect commercial fishers directly with retail buyers in a local geography. By keeping sales local, the need for intermediaries is reduced and commercial fishers can make more revenue for every fish they catch. Currently, we provide inventory management and traceability software for large companies in the industry at an affordable price.

Coastline's automated workflow eliminates the need for manual data re-entry, with set conversion algorithms tracking the weight, catch date, location, and product-state changes for the fishery. Further, the platform is developing a solution to provide fisheries an ability to connect with government catch-data servers. By connecting with government data servers, Coastline ensures true traceability and can increase the value of our client's inventory.
